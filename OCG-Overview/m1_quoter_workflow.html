Why this view exists

The existing M1 ERP · Quote Module ERD shows the shape of M1's quote data — seven qm* tables and how they relate. This page shows the contents: ten read-only views into a historical M1 quote, so a quoter can see everything the record holds without opening M1 itself.

Every view is anchored to Quote 1671 — a real piping-fittings assembly kit quote (customer 64225, part 51812 rev 21000, dated 2000-12-08). Q1671's header and line items are extracted directly from m1_CG.mdf; operations, materials, memos, assemblies, and price breaks are schema-confirmed but illustrative values.

This viewer is read-only. No records are created, duplicated, or edited — it surfaces what is already in M1 for easier review. Phase A (this page) is the full-record view for one quote. Phase B (coming) is the search/lookup experience across 42,000+ historical M1 quotes.

Relationship map · qm* table fan-out
qmp (Quotes, PK: QuoteID)
 ├── qml (QuoteLines)          1 quote → many lines       key: QuoteID + QuoteLineID
 │    ├── qmo (Operations)     1 line → many operations   key: QuoteID + QuoteLineID
 │    ├── qmm (Materials)      1 line → many materials    key: QuoteID + QuoteLineID
 │    └── qma (Assemblies)     1 line → 1+ assembly node  key: QuoteID + QuoteLineID
 ├── qmq (Quantities)          1 quote → many price breaks key: QuoteID
 └── qmk (Memos)               1 quote → many memos        key: QuoteID
