Overton Foundation  +  Overton Intake

Phases 1–2 of the OCG modernization. Foundation is the authenticated lookup surface that joins M1 quote estimates to ProfitKey production actuals. Intake is the Phase 2 front door: RFQ PDFs upload, AI Vision extracts into M1's input shape, and tolerance-sensitive specs get flagged for estimator sign-off before handoff to M1.

Phases 1–2 of 4
10 screens · 1 hub
Read-only
Read-only This app is a lookup surface, not an editor. No records are created, duplicated, or modified. Source-of-record remains with M1 and ProfitKey; the staging layer is a nightly read-only extract.
